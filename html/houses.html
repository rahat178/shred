Accommodation in the cabins.

There are 8 cabins in total. All the cabins are identical. On one side, there are large panoramic windows with lanterns and terraces. The terraces and panoramic windows are positioned in such a way that you can't see your neighbors, only the forest.

  • Extra beds are 50$ per person
  • Children under 5 years old stay for free
  • Children under 16 years old - 25$
  • A baby cot – playpen with baby bed linens – 50$ (provided upon request at least 24 hours in advance – please call us or email)
  • Pets are allowed, and accommodation for animals is free of charge
  • Free parking on the premises

In each cabin:

  • 2 floors (loft layout)
  • A double bed, bed linens, towels
  • A double sofa bed
  • A hammock
  • Shower, toilet, hairdryer, hot water
  • Underfloor heating, heater
  • Equipped kitchen: all dishes, stove, refrigerator, kettle
  • Clothing wardrobe
  • Dining table
  • Panoramic glazing with a view of the forest
  • A terrace with chairs
  • Barbecue, outdoor table

The cabin accommodates up to 4 guests, including children!
