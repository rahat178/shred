Shred of happiness - your dream house in the forest

Eight individual cabins with panoramic views of a pine forest, located 40 km from Seattle.

In the mornings, it’s wonderful to wake up with the first rays of sunlight, take walks in the pine grove, read books, listen to birds, relax in the warm bath, or enjoy a riverside sauna. In the evenings, you can gaze at the stars and sit by the fire. It’s an ideal place to come with family, as a couple, or even alone, to embrace the quiet and escape from the busy city. Once you’ve been here, you’ll definitely want to return!


A Place to Fall in Love With

Not only is it beautiful and photogenic, but each cabin also has everything you need for a comfortable stay.
There’s a double bed on the second floor, and on the ground floor, you’ll find a double sofa bed, a fully equipped kitchen, a toilet, and a shower.
And pets are welcome here too, with no extra charge for their stay.




Important to know


  • Booking

    You can book online with a 50% deposit for stays of two nights or more, and full payment for one-night stays.

  • Check-In and Check-Out

    Check-in is after 4:00 PM, and check-out is by 1:00 PM. We look forward to welcoming you!

  • Cancellation Policy

    In case of a cancellation, the deposit is non-refundable. However, you can notify us 7 days in advance, and we’ll happily reschedule your booking to another convenient date free of charge.

  • Internet

    Mobile internet works great throughout the area, with LTE coverage from all major carriers.

  • Water Access

    The cabins are located by a river. Swimming is allowed in the summer, and there are fish, so feel free to bring your fishing gear!

  • Shops

    The nearest store is a 10-minute drive away.

  • Insects

    Every spring, we treat the area for ticks. In the summer, the cabins are equipped with bug strips and repellents. The only snakes around are harmless grass snakes.

  • Roads

    After leaving the main paved road, you’ll drive about 10 km on a gravel road.
